Life

CCA

National School Games representing Jurong West Secondary School. Welfare Head of Basketball for 2 years. Always cared for my team to make sure they were well and healthy. Being the best at the sport was not really my thing but I learnt to be the one who takes care of the team and keeping team morale and spirit high for important games, earning me my place as welfare head.

Friends

Through basketball I have met many new people whether it be in school or outside. Many times, people I meet from pick up basketball games at the basketball court in school or at the court nearby my block are the people that I end up being close with due to a similar interest in basketball. To whoever I played basketball with in my life, I appreciate each and everyone of you.
